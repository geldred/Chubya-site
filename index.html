Year VI

Welcome to the Chubya Cup

Welcome to Year 6 of the Chubya Cup Series — undisputed crown jewel of the western hemisphere and a league so prestigious it exudes brilliance. The glory of the Chubya Cup Series intensifies with every season, along with rivalries, trash talk, and competition.

All distinction and absolute, excessive power to the Chair — may he rule for 1,000 years with an iron fist and a shrinking handicap. Long live the Chair.

We must congratulate our 2025 Series Champion, our low-cap, our chief, Mr. Christopher Zeigler. Not only winning the Series, but doing it in back-to-back years — an incredible feat. Peter Kelly brings the Green Jacket back to the course after a decisive victory at the Alabama Masters. Ben McBeth captured his first Major with an Upstate victory.

Best of luck to all competitors as they seek the Masters Jacket. 🏆
